FAQs

+ How much can our society save with solar?

Housing societies can save 70-90% on their common area electricity bills. For a typical society, this means savings of ₹50,000 to ₹5,00,000 annually depending on the system size and consumption pattern. Most societies recover their investment within 3-5 years.

+ Is there a government subsidy for housing societies?

Yes, the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) provides subsidies for residential societies through the State Nodal Agencies. The subsidy can be up to 20-40% of the system cost depending on the system capacity and state policies.

+ How do we apply for the subsidy?

Sharabesh handles the entire subsidy application process on your behalf. We help with documentation, online application submission, coordination with DISCOM and State Nodal Agency, and subsidy claim processing.

+ Does our society need an AGM approval to install solar?

Yes, AGM approval is required as per most society bylaws. We provide complete documentation and can help present the proposal to society members, including savings calculations and ROI analysis to secure approval.

+ What is the typical payback period for a solar system?

The payback period for society solar installations is typically 3-5 years. With government subsidies and current electricity rates, some societies achieve payback in as little as 2.5 years.

+ What happens if solar generation is lower than expected?

Our systems come with a generation guarantee. We use advanced monitoring to track performance. If generation falls below committed levels, we diagnose and fix the issue at no extra cost. Our 25-year performance warranty ensures peace of mind.

+ How much rooftop space is needed for a solar installation?

Typically, 100 sq. ft. of shadow-free rooftop area generates about 1 kW of solar power. Our team conducts a detailed shadow analysis to determine the optimal placement and maximum possible capacity for your society.

+ What are the different financing options available?

We offer multiple financing options: upfront payment with subsidy, EMI plans with 0% interest options, lease models where we install at no upfront cost, and bank financing with easy repayment terms up to 5 years.

+ How do we ensure the safety and longevity of the solar installation?

Our installations use cyclone-proof mounting structures tested for 170 kmph winds. We provide a 5-year comprehensive AMC including quarterly cleaning, health checks, and repairs. Our systems come with a 25-year performance warranty.

+ What maintenance does a solar system require?

Solar systems require minimal maintenance. Our 5-year free AMC includes quarterly panel cleaning, inverter health check, performance monitoring, and electrical connection inspection. We also provide 24/7 remote monitoring.
