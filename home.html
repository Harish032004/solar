Common Doubts? We Have Answers

Everything you need to know about switching to a brighter, cleaner future.

What is the lifespan of a Sharabesh Solar System?

Our Tier-1 solar panels are designed to last for 25–30 years. We provide a 25-year performance warranty ensuring your energy production remains efficient for decades.

Will installing panels damage my roof?

Not at all. We use non-penetrative mounting structures for concrete roofs and specialized waterproof flashing for other types to ensure your roof's integrity remains 100% intact.

Can I install solar on a non-concrete roof?

Yes! We have specialized engineering solutions for tin sheds, asbestos, and tiled roofs. Our team conducts a structural audit before every installation.

What is the annual maintenance cost?

Solar systems have no moving parts, so maintenance is minimal. With Sharabesh, your first year of professional cleaning and health checks is usually included!

How often do the panels need cleaning?

For maximum efficiency in Indian conditions, we recommend cleaning the panels every 15 days with pressurized water to remove dust and pollutants.

How much can I actually save?

Most customers see a 90% reduction in their monthly bills. The typical ROI (Return on Investment) for a residential system is just 3–4 years.

Services Sharabesh Offers

#1. Site Inspection & 3D Design

Being in the game since 2016, we understand how important a site inspection is. Our solar consultants visit the installation site to determine:

  • The type of roof: RCC or a metal sheet roof.
  • The structural strength and condition of the roof.
  • The size of the rooftop.
  • Ideal tilt angle and direction for the solar panels.

Every single parameter is taken into account to locate the ideal spot on your rooftop – a spot that receives unhindered sunlight between 9 AM to 4 PM.
